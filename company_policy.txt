TECHCORP COMPANY POLICY DOCUMENT
==================================

SECTION 1: REFUND POLICY
--------------------------
TechCorp offers a 30-day money-back guarantee on all products.
To request a refund, customers must contact support@techcorp.com
with their order ID and reason for refund.
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.
Digital products are non-refundable after download.
Subscription plans can be cancelled anytime but are non-refundable
for the current billing period.

SECTION 2: SHIPPING POLICY
----------------------------
TechCorp ships to over 50 countries worldwide.
Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days.
Express shipping takes 1-2 business days and costs $15 extra.
Free shipping is available on orders above $100.
International orders may take 10-15 business days.
Customers receive a tracking number via email once shipped.

SECTION 3: LEAVE POLICY
-------------------------
Full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of paid leave per year.
Sick leave is 10 days per year and does not carry over.
Maternity leave is 6 months fully paid.
Paternity leave is 2 weeks fully paid.
Leave requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance.
Emergency leaves are approved on a case-by-case basis by HR.
Unused leave can be carried over up to a maximum of 10 days.

SECTION 4: WORK FROM HOME POLICY
----------------------------------
Employees can work from home up to 3 days per week.
Core working hours are 10 AM to 4 PM in local timezone.
Employees must be reachable on Slack during core hours.
A stable internet connection of minimum 10 Mbps is required.
Work from home is not allowed during client visits or team events.
New employees must complete 3 months before applying for WFH.

SECTION 5: PAYMENT AND BILLING
--------------------------------
TechCorp accepts Visa, Mastercard, PayPal, and bank transfers.
All prices are in USD unless stated otherwise.
Invoices are sent automatically on the billing date.
Failed payments are retried 3 times over 7 days.
Accounts with failed payments are suspended after 14 days.
To update payment method, go to Settings > Billing > Payment Method.

SECTION 6: CUSTOMER SUPPORT
-----------------------------
Support is available Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM IST.
Email support: support@techcorp.com (response within 24 hours)
Live chat is available on the website during business hours.
Phone support: +1-800-TECHCORP (Premium customers only)
Priority support is included in Enterprise plans.
Average response time for premium customers is under 2 hours.

SECTION 7: DATA PRIVACY
-------------------------
TechCorp does not sell customer data to third parties.
All data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption.
Customers can request data deletion by emailing privacy@techcorp.com.
Data is stored in servers located in the United States and Europe.
TechCorp is GDPR compliant for European customers.
Annual security audits are conducted by third-party firms.

SECTION 8: EMPLOYEE CODE OF CONDUCT
--------------------------------------
Employees must maintain professional behavior at all times.
Harassment of any kind is strictly prohibited and grounds for termination.
Confidential company information must not be shared externally.
Conflicts of interest must be disclosed to HR immediately.
Employees must complete annual compliance training.
Personal use of company equipment is limited to 30 minutes per day.
